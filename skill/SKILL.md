# crypto-legal-skill

AI legal guidance for Solana founders and builders — from incorporation and token launches to KYC/AML compliance and emerging market regulations.

> This skill does not provide formal legal advice. It provides structured, research-backed guidance to help founders ask better questions and move faster with their legal counsel.

## When to load this skill

Load this skill when the user asks about:
- How to incorporate or structure a crypto company
- Whether their token is a security
- KYC/AML obligations for their project
- Stablecoin regulations (issuing or integrating)
- DAO legal wrappers and governance
- Crypto regulations in specific countries
- Compliance for launching on Solana

## Quick answers (no sub-file needed)

**"Do I need a lawyer?"**
Yes, for anything you'll actually sign or launch. This skill helps you prepare — not replace — legal counsel.

**"Which jurisdiction is best for a Solana startup?"**
Cayman Islands (foundations), BVI (holding companies), UAE (DIFC/ADGM), Wyoming (DAOs), Marshall Islands (DAOs) — depends on your investor profile, team location, and token type. Read `incorporation.md`.

**"Is my token a security?"**
Depends on the Howey Test + your specific structure. Read `token-launch-legal.md` before you launch anything.

**"I'm based in Pakistan / Nigeria / Turkey — can I even do this?"**
Yes, many founders from these countries operate globally through offshore entities. Read `emerging-markets.md`.
